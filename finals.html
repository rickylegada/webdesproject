Minecraft is a game about building blocks with a twist of java programming that could modify the game as much as you like. With this, it has opened up unlimited of potential for market. It is an emmersive game with basically giving ability to do anything that you'd like, making FPS, RPG or classic PvP

The community of minecraft is run by dedicated servers that is running on powerful processors and ram to deliver the best possible experience. This made a lot of people to open up their own hosting companies to cater the demand of the community

What makes people make their own server? money...

Profiting has became a norm on the community, some even made millions of dollars from it. This shows the potential that you are able to do in the game. By alterning and putting the ideas into your server and making it unique than anyone else out there which gives you the edge from all others. People would buy or pay on your server as long as you made it fun and entertaining for them. That is just how people are, as long as they enjoy and have fun, they would not care about money anymore.

It might sound so appealing with having to earn money by playing the game however this has invited some unwanted people unto the game such as scammers, hackers and fraud. These risk has been at large since the game was released however it did not stop any of the playes from making their servers for the fact that the advantage of making one overshadows of not making it.
